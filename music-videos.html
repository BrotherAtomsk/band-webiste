Music Videos

Yxngxr1 is quickly becoming an upcoming staple of the indie-rap genre. Hailing from Cardiff the artist boasts an evident DIY ethos with many of his six albums being self-made. Now, on the day he releases his latest LP Teenage Motel, we are introduced to his lively lead single “BMW”. Since 2019, the Welshman Yxngxr1 has been an integral part of the rap landscape. With his debut record “Tyler” he caused a stir and reached millions of streams within a short time. But despite his intoxicating success, he continued to challenge himself and experimented with all genres in the studio. His greatest role model is Lil Peep, who inspires him both musically and visually.
