How'd He Began

British-born Timothy Francis Lee, better known as yxngxr1, is a singer and rapper from Cardiff, Wales, currently signed to American record label EMPIRE.yxngxr1 has made it no secret that American artists Tyler, the Creator and Dominic Fike are people he admires, who thereby possibly inspire his music.The singer started gaining on SoundCloud in early 2019 with singles like “Tyler” and “RockStore.” His full-length debut, Childhood Dreams, arrived in October the same year. In March 2020, he released an eight-track project entitled I Don’t Suit Hats.
